Jameela Jamil is an English actress, radio presenter, model, writer and activist. She began her career on T4, where she hosted a pop culture series from 2009 to 2012. Shen then became the radio host of The Offical Chart, and was co-host of The Offical Chart Update on BBC Radio 1. She was the first solo female presenter of the BBC Radio 1 chart show.

In 2016, she moved to the United States, and was cast as Tahani Al-Jamil in the NBC fantasy comedy series, The Good Place. She has modelled for British and American Vogue, Glamour, Cosmopolitan, and Teen Vogue, and has written for The Times, Cosmopolitan, The Huffington Post, and Company.

In March 2018, Jamil created an Instagram account, I Weigh , inspired by a picture of Kourtney, Kim and Khloe Kardashian, along with Kendall and Kylie Jenner, detailing each woman's weight. The account welcomes submission of follower's non-edited selfies using the hashtage #iweigh, with text describing the things they feel grateful or proud of.
